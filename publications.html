The refuge effect of humid microhabitats for ferns decreases towards more arid regions

2026

Paez, S., Aros-Mualin, D., Kessler, M., Kluge, J., and Ismael Meza-Torres, E.

Frontiers in Ecology and Evolution 14:1719536.

DOI
Abstract summary

This study compares fern diversity in zonal and humid azonal habitats across northeastern Argentina and finds that microhabitat refugia support richer fern communities only where broader moisture conditions remain favorable.
