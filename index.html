Fern and lycophyte ecophysiology

How ancient plant lineages live with light, water, and place.

Daniela Aros-Mualin studies the physiological and ecological mechanisms that shape fern and lycophyte diversity. Her work connects gas exchange, stomatal regulation, desiccation tolerance, microhabitat refugia, and the evolution of independent plant generations.
